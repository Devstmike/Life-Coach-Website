What Does a Life Coach Do?

A life coach encourages and guides clients on a range of professional and personal issues. Life coaches help you uncover the information about yourself that you never knew existed or that you have never felt comfortable sharing with others.

During the process, we will analyze and break down your current situation, discover limiting beliefs, identify any blocks and obstacles you are facing, and build a plan of action to help you access the outcomes and life that you crave.

A life coach won’t force any diagnosis or “cure all” answer. As a client, you get the full opportunity to discover what it is you want to change or enhance in your life, and how to do it in ways that work for you.

The life coaching approach is not giving advice, consulting, counseling, mentoring and therapy. A coach is more action-oriented and would be hired to help with personal and professional projects, goals and/or transitions.

Benefits of Having a Life Coach

  • Gain clarity on your purpose and lock-in on the goals that will radically change your life
  • Learn techniques to cultivate a healthy body and mind for sustained success
  • Monitor the progress of your goals with weekly check-ins and feedback from Taylor
  • Reframe limiting beliefs and develop a proactive mindset to help overcome insecurities and fear
  • Learn how to stick to goals and create action plans to achieve the results you are looking for
  • One-on-one coaching emphasizing support, encouragement and accountability
